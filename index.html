Welcome to Data Science for Manufacturing

Explore core data science concepts tailored to the manufacturing domain. The course emphasizes data collection, preparation, analysis, visualization, and applied analytics that improve manufacturing processes and decision-making. Through theory and hands-on Python workshops, you will work with industrial data, build reproducible analyses, and communicate insights that drive value on the shop floor and beyond.

Prerequisites

This course has no prerequisites. Participants are not required to have prior knowledge of coding or data science. Designed with industry professionals in mind, it introduces the fundamentals of data-driven analysis in manufacturing, regardless of technical background. If you are new to Python or analytics, the workshops will guide you step by step.

Structure of the Programme / Course Outline

We adopt a modular approach to cover essential data science topics for manufacturing, combining concepts with hands-on Python practice across progressive weekly themes.

Course Topics

  • Week 1: Introduction
  • Week 2: Data Carpentry
  • Week 3: Product Lifecycle / Material Flows
  • Week 4: Data Visualization & Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
  • Week 5: Data Cleaning & EDA
  • Week 6: Current Manufacturing Software (Databases)
  • Week 7: Version Control — Introduction to Git, commands, GitHub
  • Week 8: Applied Analytics, Digital Twins & IoT
  • Week 9: Process Mining / PCA
  • Week 10: 3D Data and Point Clouds

Meet Our Team

The AI in Manufacturing team at NMIS combines expertise in data science, machine learning, and industrial engineering to advance manufacturing through intelligent systems.

AI in Manufacturing Team at NMIS

The AI in Manufacturing team is part of the National Manufacturing Institute Scotland (NMIS), a pioneering research and innovation center dedicated to transforming the manufacturing industry. Our team focuses on developing and deploying cutting-edge artificial intelligence and data science solutions that address real-world manufacturing challenges. From predictive maintenance to digital twins, from process optimization to quality control, we work closely with industry partners to bridge the gap between academic research and practical industrial applications.
